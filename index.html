Ad Free

How frustrating is a website full of annoying ads that take up space and slow down load times?

We wouldn't know...

Our free gaming websites are also Ad Free. Ads on our competitors websites take up 50% - 80% of the screen above the fold.

Forums

Forums are the lifeblood of every gaming website. We've developed our forum software from scratch with the intent to make them powerful and intuitive.

Forums use our innovative customizable widget framework, meaning you can change every single aspect of them to meet your needs.

Theme Builder

Create a beautiful and unique website theme in seconds with just a few clicks. The Theme Builder is available to all website plans for free.

A nice looking website will help make your members experience more enjoyable. Our Border Kit color variations will help you brand your website perfectly.

Responsive Design

Every single page and widget of your website is designed to be desktop and phone friendly. It's painstaking work being this good...

Our page designer lets you define how columns break down on different viewing devices so you can control what your website looks like accross all devices.

Customize Widgets

Premium websites have complete control over the widget code and styling. Modify your forum layout, your navigation structure, or your roster display. There is no limit to what you can do with our widgets!

Every widget is built using the amazing Vue.js 2 framework and our Guildtag API. You have the ability to modify the widget code any way you want. Check out the wiki article on customizing widgets in the support forum and start tinkering!

Security First Approach

Security is important to us, and it should be to you too!

Create user specific roles and set permissions to control who can see forums and widgets on your website.

All websites are secured by our *.guildtag.com SSL certificate. Premium websites that want to use their own custom domain get a free SSL certificate through Let's Encrypt.

Points System

Website points are a website specific currency mainly used to purchase items from your Shop. You could also use points as a merit tracking system, or to track raid loot distribution.

Tasks

Create automated tasks that can assign points, send messages, and add/remove roles from website users based on conditions being met

Tasks can be set to trigger again after a period of time has elapsed to create recurring tasks.

Wiki

For organizations that have a lot of information they want to share - our Wiki allows everyone in the Wiki Role to add and edit pages.

Our Wiki is another customizable widget, so you can change it to do anything you want.

Commerce Shop

Build a shop where you can sell items to your website members in exchange for points.

Website points can be assigned manually or through automated tasks when conditions are met.

Twitch Integration

Guildtag pulls in data from Twitch for all of your website members who provide their Twitch Account. Display and customize widgets that show streamers stats and current streaming details!

Discord Integration

Use our discord bot to integrate your website with your Discord Server. Members can create a guildtag account directly from Discord. Command the bot to show leaderboards, sync roles, and roll dice! Allow members to register for events with roles using reactions in discord.

Push news and events and applications to your server easily with discord webhooks.

Tracking

Our tracking widget gives you a way to track, plan, communicate, and discuss changes on large projects.

Members can provide feedback and spend website points to vote on items they want to have higher priority.

Awards

Awards are assigned to members and can be used in various ways. From assigning Ranks/Badges without using Roles, to awarding members for their organization achievements.

Awards are also baked into our Tasks system so that members can automatically be assigned awards when specific conditions are met.

Social Profiles

Not only does your gaming community get an awesome new website, they also get access to our integrated Social Network which helps to keep them engaged with your website and Guildtag.

Users can add characters, blogs, reviews, images, and more to their social profile. Much of this content can be integrated directly into your website via widgets (roster, events, image gallery, blogs...) to make it more robust.
